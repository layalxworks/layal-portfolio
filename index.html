About

The person behind the models

I'm an Artificial Intelligence student who likes to see models leave the notebook. Across my projects I've trained a robot to recognize and sort objects in real time, compared classic machine learning against an LLM-based approach for sentiment analysis, and restored noisy images using classical signal processing — the common thread is turning a learning algorithm into something that actually does something.

I move comfortably across the AI stack: computer vision with TensorFlow and OpenCV, NLP with TF-IDF and prompt engineering, classic machine learning with Scikit-learn, and robotics with Webots. I care as much about getting a model to 96% accuracy as I do about what happens after — deploying it, testing it, and making sure it holds up outside the training set.

My goal is to grow into a well-rounded AI Engineer: someone who's equally comfortable training a model, reasoning about its limitations, and shipping it as part of a real system.
